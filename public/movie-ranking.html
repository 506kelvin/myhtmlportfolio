ALL TIME FAVOURITE MOVIES

Top 3 in the list

1. Lupin

This movie involves a character who engages himself in well thought out robberies. He's targets are mostly the most expensive jewels. The movie is based in France. The main character disguises himself in the name Lupin which he got from a novel book that his father gave to him. The police in France really have a hard time finding Lupin but eventually on the last seasom of the movie they do, but only because he decides to surrender.

2. Shooting stars

This movie tells of the rise of the famous basketball star Lebron James. The environment he grew in is what stood out for me, especially having been raised by a single mother

3. The beekeeper

This movie is acted by Jason Statham as the main character. He has just quit his job as a secret service agent named as the "beekeepers" in the US. He is hosted by a very wonderful old lady, but one day as Jason Statham goes to have dinner at her house, he finds her dead body after she commits suicide. The movie is about Jason Statham finding out why th old lady does this and who are behind it.
